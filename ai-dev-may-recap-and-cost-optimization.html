Infrastructure Strategy
Decoupling Transcription from Analytics
Simplify Transcription
Zoom Native + Splain
We are already paying for Zoom. Instead of outsourcing recordings/transcripts to external bots at variable per-hour rates:
• Capture recordings and transcripts using Zoom Native Cloud.
• Use Splain to move files directly from Zoom cloud to Google Drive.
Move to Google Workspace
Google Apps Script Automations
Google Workspace is underutilized. We are moving n8n's Google Drive organization tasks to Apps Script.
• Better, direct handling of Google Drive.
• Eases the maintenance burden of n8n integrations.
• Eliminates n8n credential bugs.
Self-Host Autobot & n8n on Hetzner VPS
To eliminate high AWS costs and bypass Railway's limits, we are rewriting a bare-bones Autobot to host on our Hetzner VPS (flat $13/mo). This reduces maintenance time and frees up hours to assist other team members with GoHighLevel automations.

Personal Reflection
Development Mindset vs. Operational Reality
"In my first two months here, I've learned that proposing changes in a corporate environment is vastly different from doing so in a pure development space."
The Dev Environment
Designed for Disruption
Developers are surrounded by technical peers. We experiment constantly, break things to learn, and can pivot tools instantly because no active client calls or daily operations are on the line.
The Operations Reality
Disruption is Costly
Operational departments are extremely busy managing client relationships and daily deliverables. Jarring tool changes introduce friction, resistance, and business risks.
The Compromise
As a developer, I must design proposals that preserve existing workflows as much as possible, minimize friction, and respect the operational tempo. We will reach our data milestones through smooth, well-planned transitions.
