Infrastructure Strategy
Decoupling Transcription from Analytics
Simplify Transcription
Zoom Native + Splain
Setting up third-party bots in n8n is highly complex and time-consuming. Shifting to native tools offers a much cleaner workflow:
• Zoom Native automatically records on start—no bots to invite or manage.
• **Splain** smoothly transfers files from Zoom cloud to Google Drive.
Move to Google Workspace
Google Apps Script Automations
We are shifting remaining file organization and cleanup tasks from n8n to Google Apps Script. This native integration yields:
• Better, direct handling of Google Drive files.
• Elimination of complex n8n workflows and credential bugs.
• A completely hands-off automation pipeline.
Zero Maintenance Goal
Sunsetting Autobot (AWS) & n8n Workflows
We are completely decommissioning the custom AWS Autobot stack and shutting down n8n workflows. By shifting to Zoom Native + Splain, the entire transcription and upload pipeline becomes 100% hands-off. This eliminates the custom code maintenance burden, freeing up developer hours to assist with automation pipelines across the company, support GHL automations, and focus on core data engineering (Nexus v2).

Personal Reflection
The Adoption Challenge & Operational Fragility
Adoption & Timing
Deliberate Polish Before Launching
Introducing tool changes in a busy operational environment requires care. To respect team bandwidth and prevent testing friction, we are intentionally polishing the platform to be smooth and stable before rolling it out. Delivering a bug-free experience ensures we do not waste operational time.
Pipeline Fragility
Google Sheet Dependencies
We successfully structured data for After-Sales, but the pipeline is still fragile because it relies on Google Sheets. If formats or column headers change on their end, the pipeline immediately breaks. We must migrate from sheets to a robust database.
Data Fragmentation Cost
Connecting Scattered Platforms Takes Too Long
Having company data split between GoHighLevel and multiple Google Sheets makes pipelines complex to maintain. True data unification is critical to eliminate manual integration overhead, keep data consistent, and enable reliable, long-term analytics.
Active Strategy
Strategic Roadmap: Custom CRM & AI Onboarding
A Custom CRM From Scratch
Designing for WeAssist's Unique Data
Because no off-the-shelf platform fits our custom operational data structure, we are building a tailored CRM from scratch. This is a significant undertaking that takes time. To ensure stability, we are scoping the initial platform strictly to 2026 data first, holding off on legacy imports.
AI-Powered Onboarding
Embedded Support Agents
The final piece of preparation before testing begins is embedding **AI Onboarding Agents** inside the app. These agents will guide users and handle support questions automatically, keeping the transition hands-off and reducing 1-on-1 support overhead.
